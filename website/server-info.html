Connection Details

To join RavenMC, you'll need Minecraft Java Edition version 1.20 or higher. Use the following server address to connect:

play.RavenMC.com

Connection Steps:

  1. Open Minecraft Java Edition
  2. Click on "Multiplayer"
  3. Click "Add Server"
  4. Enter "RavenMC" as the server name
  5. Enter "play.RavenMC.com" as the server address
  6. Click "Done" and then select the server to join

First time joining?

When you first join RavenMC, you'll spawn in our welcome hub. Follow the tutorial to learn about our unique raven-themed leveling system and how to get started.

Server Rules

To maintain a positive and enjoyable environment for all players, we ask everyone to follow these rules:

1. Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, and bullying are not tolerated. Keep chat appropriate and friendly.

2. No Griefing or Stealing

Respect other players' builds and property. Do not destroy, modify, or take items from other players without permission, even if their land is not claimed.

3. No Hacking or Exploits

Using hacked clients, mods that give unfair advantages, or exploiting bugs to gain an edge is prohibited. Allowed mods include Optifine, minimaps, and cosmetic mods.

4. PvP Restrictions

PvP is only allowed in designated areas or during events. Forced PvP, spawn camping, and combat logging are not permitted.

5. Keep Chat Family-Friendly

Maintain appropriate language in all chat channels. Excessive swearing, adult content, and inappropriate discussions are not allowed.

6. No Spamming or Advertising

Do not spam chat with repeated messages or excessive caps. Advertising other servers or unrelated content is prohibited.

7. Respect Server Performance

Avoid building lag-inducing machines or contraptions that could negatively impact server performance for other players.

Rule Enforcement

Our moderation team enforces these rules with a strike system:

  • First offense: Warning
  • Second offense: Temporary mute or kick
  • Third offense: Temporary ban (1-7 days)
  • Severe or repeated offenses: Permanent ban

Staff have final discretion on rule interpretation and enforcement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start leveling up my Raven?

When you first join, you'll receive a Raven Egg. Right-click it to hatch your companion. Complete daily quests, mine resources, defeat enemies, and build structures to gain XP. As you level up, your raven will evolve and gain new abilities.

Can I claim land to protect my builds?

Yes! Use the /claim command to start protecting your land. New players receive 2,000 claim blocks initially, and you earn more as you play. You can add trusted players to your claim with /trust [playername].

How does the economy work?

Our economy is based on Raven Tokens. You earn tokens by completing quests, participating in events, trading with other players, and selling items at the community marketplace. Use tokens to purchase special items, claim more land, or upgrade your raven abilities.

Is there a teleport system?

Yes, we have several teleport options:
- /spawn - Return to the server spawn
- /home - Teleport to your set home points (unlock more as you level up)
- /warp - Visit server warps like shopping district, events, and more
- /tpa - Request to teleport to another player

What makes RavenMC different from other servers?

RavenMC offers a unique Raven-themed leveling experience you won't find elsewhere. Your companion raven grows with you, gaining abilities that help with mining, combat, exploration, and more. We also offer custom biomes, structures, enchantments, and regular community events designed around our raven theme.

Do I need to use Discord to play?

While not required, joining our Discord server is highly recommended. We announce events, updates, and maintenance there, and it's the best place to get support if you need help. Plus, linking your Minecraft account to Discord gives you additional perks!

How often do you reset the map?

We aim to keep the main world running for 6-8 months before considering a reset. When resets happen, we provide ample notice and allow players to download their builds. Player progress, including raven levels and economy, carries over to new maps.
